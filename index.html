Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2023-12-17

Introduction

Skate Rank is a mobile application that allows users to keep track of their skateboarding tricks and receive a score. This score is posted on a globale leaderboard of the other app users. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about our users.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information from our users. This includes your name (you can input whatever name you want in the app, it doesn't have to be your real name), email address, location, or any other information that could be used to identify you.

Information Use

We do not use any information about our users.

Information Sharing

We do not share any information about our users with any third parties.

Security

We take the security of our users' information very seriously. We do not store any information about our users on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for any age. No data is collected regardless of age.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on our discord server or in the app.

Contact Us
